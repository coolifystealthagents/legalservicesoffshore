# Design summary

- Design concept: HR policy desk
- Layout personality: compact procedural
- Typography: Merriweather headings with Public Sans body text
- Color palette: primary #4c1d95, secondary #a78bfa, accent #f59e0b, background #faf5ff, text #1f1b2e, muted #6b7280
- Shape language: Mixed geometric shapes
- Visual motif: tabs, stamps, and policy boxes
- Unique layout choices: first sections use Compliance desk, then a custom service index, process ribbon, question block, and resource strip with varied widths and alignments.
- Difference from previous sites: this redesign changes the hero composition, section ordering, image treatment, card style, spacing rhythm, button shape, and footer treatment for this domain.
